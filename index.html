About This Simulator

This tool is pre-loaded with the current 2025 F1 driver standings. You can use it to simulate the results for all remaining Grands Prix and Sprint races to see how the final standings will change.

How it Works:

  • The "Status" column automatically calculates if a driver has Clinched the title or been Eliminated from contention.
  • The "Max Points Left" includes all points from remaining Grands Prix (26 per race) and Sprints (8 per sprint).
  • Click "Reset to Start of Season" to clear your simulations and return to the current, real-world standings.
